Who am I & What do i do

I am a Multimedia Engineer with experience in developing interactive applications, particularly in Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR) environments. I have worked on creating simulations, training experiences, and digital tools using technologies such as Unity, C#, and C++. My professional focus is on building immersive solutions that combine technology, learning, and usability, while continuously strengthening my skills in software development and interactive system design.

I am motivated by creating solutions that have a real impact on how people learn and train, especially through virtual environments and simulations. I enjoy solving technical challenges, optimizing systems, and turning complex ideas into functional and user-friendly experiences. I am particularly interested in simulation development, training tools, and interactive applications that integrate creativity with technical precision.

Outside of work, I am interested in technology related to video games and simulations, and I enjoy exploring new tools and mechanics that can improve user experience. I continue developing personal projects and expanding my technical skills while seeking new opportunities to grow professionally.
